A live needs-map that matches surplus resources to local organizations in real time.

SF Tech Week Hackathon November 2025 Developer and designer Pitched live at SF Tech Week
01Problem

People have surplus and the will to give, but not the information: what is needed, where to take it, and when an organization is accepting drop-offs. That gap turns into waste while pantries stay under-served.

02Insight
Generosity is mostly a routing problem.

Make the need legible and nearby and the donation follows.

03What I built

A data-centric prototype on Gemini Canvas with a live map that aggregates real-time requests and lets users filter by category and distance, plus a retention layer, a community heat map and a friends leaderboard, to gamify neighborhood support.
